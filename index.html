About
Me

Hello and welcome to my online portfolio! My name is Temuulen Tsengel, but everybody calls me Tem, and I go by she/her pronouns. I am currently enrolled in the UofT Edx Full Stack Development Bootcamp, where I am learning to code and become a web developer! I will continue to update this page to reflect my work and projects as I complete them.

A little bit about me: I am from a country called Mongolia, where I was born and raised. I came to Canada when I was in high school, and lived in Vancouver for a while before eventually moving to Toronto to complete my undergraduate studies. I graduated from University of Toronto with an Honour's Bachelor of Arts in 2021, and have since been working as a full-time coordinator for the University. A random fact about me: I help out my sister in running her small business named Solly's Library where she sells handmade crochet items, and appears at pop-up markets around downtown Toronto!
